**Parameters and their permissible limits:**

1. **PM2.5**
   - Description: Particulate matter less than 2.5 µm in aerodynamic equivalent diameter.

2. **Point source**
   - Description: Source of emissions of odour, generally a stack. Emissions can generally be relatively easily controlled by using waste reduction, waste minimisation and cleaner production principles or conventional emission control equipment.

3. **Sensitive receptor**
   - Description: A location where people are likely to work or reside; this may include a dwelling, school, hospital, office or public recreational area. An air quality impact assessment should also consider the location of known or likely future sensitive receptors. For hydrogen fluoride, a sensitive receptor includes land-use areas with vegetation sensitive to hydrogen fluoride, such as grapevines and stone fruit.

4. **Separation distance**
   - Description: The distance between a source and sensitive receptors (or likely future sensitive receptors).

5. **Source separation**
   - Description: The distance between two emission sources.

6. **σ (Standard deviation)**
   - Description: Standard deviation.

7. **σy**
   - Description: Initial horizontal plume spread for volume sources.

8. **σz**
   - Description: Initial vertical plume spread for volume sources.

9. **Stack**
   - Description: A vertical pipe used to vent pollutants from a process.

10. **Stationary source**
    - Description: Any premises-based activity; does not include motor vehicles.

11. **TAPM**
    - Description: CSIRO’s PC-based, 3-D prognostic model for air pollution studies.

12. **Total impact**
    - Description: The total impact of an emission source (or group of sources) and existing ambient levels of a pollutant; i.e. total impact = background levels + incremental impact.

13. **TSP (Total suspended particulate matter)**
    - Description: Total suspended particulate matter.

14. **USEPA**
    - Description: United States Environmental Protection Agency.

15. **VOC (Volatile organic compound)**
    - Description: Volatile organic compound.

16. **Peak-to-mean ratio**
    - Description: A conversion factor that adjusts mean dispersion model predictions to the peak concentrations perceived by the human nose.

17. **PEF (Potency equivalency factor)**
    - Description: Potency equivalency factor.

18. **pphm**
    - Description: Concentration in parts per hundred million.

19. **ppm**
    - Description: Concentration in parts per million.

20. **PM10**
    - Description: Particulate matter less than 10 µm in aerodynamic equivalent diameter.
